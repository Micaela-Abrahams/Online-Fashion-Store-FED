Returns Policy:

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Dash Sneakers.
If for any reason you're not happy with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return or exchange, your item must be in its original condition and packaging, with all tags attached.
You'll also need to include a copy of your order confirmation or packing slip.

To initiate a return or exchange, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account on our website and navigate to the Returns & Exchanges page.
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return or exchange and follow the prompts to generate a return shipping label.
  3. Pack your item(s) securely in the original packaging, and attach the return shipping label to the outside of the box.
  4. Drop off your package at a shipping carrier of your choice.

Once we receive your returned item(s), we'll inspect them to ensure they're in their original condition.
If everything checks out, we'll issue a refund to your original payment method or process an exchange within 5-7 business days.

Please note that shipping costs for returns or exchanges are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item(s) received were defective or incorrect.
In that case, please contact us immediately so we can make it right.

If you have any additional questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us through our website or social media channels.
We're always here to help!
